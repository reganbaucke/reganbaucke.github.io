Regan Baucke

Post Doctoral Researcher

CERMICS Laboratory at École des Ponts

I am a post doctoral researcher in the field of Mathematical Programming and Optimisation. Currently, I am part of the optimisation team in the CERMICS laboratory at École des Ponts, France. Before then, I completed my PhD in my home country of New Zealand; my thesis focused on risk aversion in multistage stochastic optimisation problems, and can be found here.

My research spans many areas of stochastic optimisation – in particular, developing and analysing algorithms for large scale problems involving uncertainty and multiple stages.

Interests

  • Stochastic programming
  • Robust optimisation
  • Applications in optimal water reservoir managment and finance
